There's something wonderful about writing a first post. It's a blank canvas,
an open road, a new repository with zero commits. Everything is possible and
nothing has gone wrong yet.

## Why another blog?

I've been writing software for a long time now. Over the years I've
accumulated thoughts, patterns, war stories, and the occasional strong opinion
about how we build things. Most of those ideas lived in Slack threads,
pull request descriptions, and design docs that disappeared into the void.

This blog is an attempt to give those ideas a more permanent home.

## What to expect

I plan to write about:

- **Systems design** — the architecture decisions that matter and the ones
  that don't (yet)
- **Engineering culture** — what makes teams productive, and what quietly
  kills momentum
- **Technical leadership** — the craft of leading without losing touch
  with the code
- **The craft itself** — code patterns, performance, reliability, and
  the joy of getting things right
